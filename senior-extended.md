# Senior kontent kengaytmasi

Quyida Senior darajasi uchun asosiy mavzular: strategic planning, team building, enterprise architecture, advanced system design va cross-functional leadership. Har biri uchun maqsadlar, amaliy mashqlar va resurslar.

## 1. Strategic Planning
Maqsad: uzoq muddatli texnik yo'nalish va biznes strategiyasi bilan muvofiqlashtirish.

Learning objectives:
- Tech roadmap tuzish va biznes maqsadlar bilan bog'lash
- Tech debt prioritization va investitsiya qarorlari
- Innovatsiyalar va R&D yondashuvini qo'llash

Exercises:
- 1 yil uchun high-level tech roadmap yozing: prioritetlar, milestones, resurslar.

Resources:
- Team Topologies, books & articles

---

## 2. Team Building & Culture
Maqsad: mustahkam jamoa va sog'lom ish madaniyati yaratish.

Learning objectives:
- Hiring va onboarding jarayonlarini optimallashtirish
- Performance review va career development
- Psychological safety va inkluziv muhit yaratish

Exercises:
- Hiring checklist va onboarding plan tuzing.

Resources:
- Google Re:Work, articles on hiring and culture

---

## 3. Enterprise Architecture
Maqsad: korporativ darajadagi tizimlarni dizayn qilish va integratsiyalash.

Learning objectives:
- Integration patterns, service orchestration, legacy modernization
- Governance, compliance, scalability at enterprise level
- Migration strategies and backward compatibility

Exercises:
- Katta legacy modul uchun modernization plan tuzing (steps, risks, rollback plan).

Resources:
- Martin Fowler, enterprise architecture patterns

---

## 4. Advanced System Design
Maqsad: global scale tizimlar uchun mustahkam, fault-tolerant dizayn.

Learning objectives:
- Global distribution, replication, sharding
- Fault tolerance, disaster recovery, SLA/ SLO definition
- Observability: logging, metrics, tracing

Exercises:
- Design a highly available service for 1M req/sec: outline components and trade-offs.

Resources:
- Designing Data-Intensive Applications

---

## 5. Cross-functional Leadership
Maqsad: Product, Design va Biznes bilan birga ishlash orqali kompaniya maqsadlariga erishish.

Learning objectives:
- Roadmap alignment with product strategy
- Influencing stakeholders and prioritization
- Building cross-team initiatives and OKRs

Exercises:
- Draft a cross-team initiative proposal with objectives, KPIs, and timeline.
